About Gru

Felonious Gru (or simply just "Gru") is the main protagonist in the Despicable Me. Gru is a 50-something-year-old man who wished to be the world's #1 villain before adopting his three daughters, Margo, Edith, and Agnes. He is the leader of an army of Minions.
